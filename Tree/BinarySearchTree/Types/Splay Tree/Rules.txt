1: Zig (Single Rotation):
single rotation to bring this node to the root.

2: Zig-Zig (Double Rotation):
left children or both right children.
two rotations in the same direction to bring the node closer to the root.

3: Zig-Zag (Double Rotation):
left child of a right parent or a right child of a left parent.
first rotation goes in one direction, and the second goes in the opposite direction.
